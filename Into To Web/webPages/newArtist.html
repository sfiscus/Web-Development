New Artist Application Form

For Use by Artists Wishing to Join the Arte Del Mundo Family of Artists

Provided below is a form. If you would like to begin selling your products via the Arte Del Mundo website then fill out the form. Only crafts that you have personally made will be sold via this website. The information that you provide will be used by the staff to help build a Web page about you! If confused about an error message or to avoid one scroll the mouse over the left hand side of the form. Some words will provide additional instructions. After you finish the form and submit it a staff member will contact you to discuss the data you provided. You will then be given an artistID number and a password. Wait to receive this before sending in data regarding the specifics of any of your products.
