Sean Fiscus

Like to know a little bit more about Mr. Fiscus?

Early Life

Sean Fiscus began making art at a very young age. His gift was the gift of song and he would share it with anyone who would stop to hear him. He found great joy and power through music. He was able to bring a smile to almost everyone he came in contact with. Which is a characteristic that would stay with him to this day.

Adolescence

During his adolescent years he attended a private high school which opened his mind up more to the arts. It was here that he came in contact with many teachers who would encourage him and mold him into the great artist he is today. It was here that he would meet the woman of his dreams and the woman he would eventually marry.

Adult Life

His adult life was full of struggle. He spent many years in school unsure just what exactly he wanted to choose for a career path. He desperately wanted to have a job in the arts but felt that he needed something to fall back on. So he spent many years at the University of Wisconsin until he choose to finish a degree in Computer Science. He is happily married and still continues to participate in his dream whenever he isn't busy in school. His dream would to one day star in the upcoming Hollywood film Mass Effect, as the lead role of Commander Shepard. His other dream would be to one day play Batman. Oh that already happened BECAUSE HE IS!!!
